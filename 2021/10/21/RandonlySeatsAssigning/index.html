Randomly Seats Assigning

2021-10-21

Description

In our high school, the arrangement of seats is fixed. It’s a routine that we need to switch our seats with the right ones in parallel every two weeks, but for the students who always sit in the first row, they may be near-sighted, and for the students in the last row, they can’t see the blackboard clearly and get teachers’ attention. As a result, I developed this program to help assign the seats.

The version one is writen in python, but it’s not beautiful and also hard to use. The verson two is writen in HTML, JavaScript, CSS. It is more beautiful and can adapt the resolution of different screens.

Version 1

The left side is the seats pattern in our classroom, and the right side is to control who will sit in or who will not sit in a certain row.

When clicking the “include” button, a small window will pop up and the user can select the name of students to let them sit in the first row.

Clicking the name card, the students’ name will be added to the input field.

Then, clicking the “arrange” button, the program will generate a randomly assigned seat but maintain the restriction of “include” field in the right side.

Version 2

This version is more beautiful than the previous version, and the “Arrange” button has a simple animation as well. However, the restrain that who will sit in which roll disapper, since we have never used this function.
